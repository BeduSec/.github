SECTION A (48 MARKS)
1.
Without using a calculator, simplify: \[ \frac{3\frac{1}{3} \div 2\frac{1}{2} + 1\frac{1}{5}}{2\frac{2}{3} - 1\frac{1}{6}} \] (04 marks)
2.
Factorize completely: \( 3x^2 - 12 \), and hence solve the equation \( 3x^2 - 12 = 0 \). (04 marks)
3.
In a class of 45 students, 28 like Mathematics (M) and 21 like English (E). If 5 students like neither subject, find the number of students who like both subjects. (04 marks)
4.
Express \( 0.454545... \) as a fraction in its simplest form. (04 marks)
5.
Find the equation of a line that passes through the point \( (3, -2) \) and is perpendicular to the line \( 2y + 4x = 7 \). (04 marks)
6.
The interior angle of a regular polygon is \( 144^\circ \). Find the number of sides of the polygon. (04 marks)
7.
Given that \( \mathbf{a} = \begin{pmatrix} 3 \\ -4 \end{pmatrix} \) and \( \mathbf{b} = \begin{pmatrix} -1 \\ 2 \end{pmatrix} \), find the vector \( 2\mathbf{a} + 3\mathbf{b} \) and its magnitude. (04 marks)
8.
A car travels 180 km on 15 litres of fuel. How many litres of fuel will it require to travel 420 km at the same rate? (04 marks)
9.
Solve the inequality: \( 3(x - 2) \le 5x + 4 \) and represent the solution on a number line. (04 marks)
10.
If \( \log_{10} 2 = 0.3010 \) and \( \log_{10} 3 = 0.4771 \), find the value of \( \log_{10} 18 \) without using tables. (04 marks)
11.
The volume of a sphere is \( 38,808 \text{ cm}^3 \). Find its radius. (Take \( \pi = 22/7 \)). (04 marks)
12.
A point \( P(2, 5) \) is rotated through \( 90^\circ \) clockwise about the origin. Find the coordinates of its image \( P' \). (04 marks)
SECTION B (72 MARKS)
13.
The marks scored by 50 students in a test are as follows:
Marks 20-29 30-39 40-49 50-59 60-69 70-79
Frequency 5 8 12 15 7 3
(a)
Calculate the mean mark using an assumed mean of 44.5. (06 marks)
(b)
Draw a histogram to represent the data. (04 marks)
(c)
Estimate the mode from the histogram. (02 marks)
14.
(a)
Construct a triangle \( ABC \) where \( AB = 8 \text{ cm} \), \( BC = 7 \text{ cm} \) and angle \( ABC = 60^\circ \). (05 marks)
(b)
Measure the length of \( AC \). (01 mark)
(c)
Construct a circle that passes through the vertices \( A, B, \) and \( C \). Measure its radius. (06 marks)
15.
A mountain climber moves from point \( A \) at the base of a hill to point \( B \) at the top. The bearing of \( B \) from \( A \) is \( 045^\circ \) and the angle of elevation of \( B \) from \( A \) is \( 30^\circ \). The horizontal distance between \( A \) and \( B \) is \( 500 \text{ m} \).
(a)
Find the height of the hill. (04 marks)
(b)
Calculate the actual distance the climber travels from \( A \) to \( B \). (04 marks)
(c)
If the climber then moves to point \( C \) which is \( 300 \text{ m} \) due East of \( B \), find the distance \( AC \). (04 marks)
16.
An employee's gross monthly income is \( 2,500,000 \) UGX. He is given the following tax-free allowances:
• Marriage allowance: \( 150,000 \) UGX
• Children allowance: \( 40,000 \) UGX per child for up to 4 children. (He has 3 children).
• Housing: \( 200,000 \) UGX

The tax rates are as follows:
Income Band (UGX)Tax Rate (%)
First 235,000Nil
Next 100,00010%
Next 75,00020%
Above 410,00030%
(a)
Calculate his total tax-free allowances. (03 marks)
(b)
Find his taxable income. (02 marks)
(c)
Calculate the total tax he pays per month. (07 marks)
17.
The diagram of a metallic bucket is a frustum of a cone with radii of the top and bottom circles as \( 20 \text{ cm} \) and \( 12 \text{ cm} \) respectively. The height of the bucket is \( 30 \text{ cm} \).
(a)
Calculate the capacity of the bucket in litres. (07 marks)
(b)
Find the area of the metal sheet required to make the bucket (excluding the handle). (05 marks)
18.
Solve the following simultaneous equations using the matrix method: \[ 3x + 2y = 12 \] \[ 4x - y = 5 \] (12 marks)
